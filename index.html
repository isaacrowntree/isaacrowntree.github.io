Technical Showcase - CamperMate Platform

Strategic Replatform - Significant Business Growth

As Head of Tech at CamperMate, I led a complete replatform, replacing the legacy system with six major interconnected modern systems. Using risk-mitigation strategies including Cloudflare Worker proxies to ensure zero-downtime transitions, this strategic replatform achieved remarkable business success with substantial revenue growth, large user base, and exceptional engagement metrics in just 3 years, serving Australian and New Zealand markets with enterprise SaaS solutions for municipal clients.

Business-Safe Approach: Rather than risky "big bang" system replacements that could disrupt operations, I implemented smart routing technology that gradually directed users to new systems while keeping the old ones running as backup. This ensured customers never experienced downtime or service interruptions during the transition, protecting revenue and user experience throughout the modernization process.
